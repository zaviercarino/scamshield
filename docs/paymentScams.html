Payment & Credit Card Scams

Payment and credit card scams are attempts to steal your money or financial information by pretending to be trusted companies, banks, or individuals.


Scammers often create fake payment pages, send urgent messages, or impersonate customer support representatives to convince victims to share card numbers, passwords, or send payments.

💳

Fake Payment Requests

Scammers send fake invoices, payment requests, or messages claiming you owe money for a service or purchase.

🏦

Bank & Credit Card Impersonation

A scammer pretends to be your bank or credit card company and claims there is a problem with your account.

🛒

Fake Online Stores

Scammers create websites selling popular products at unrealistic prices. After payment, victims may receive nothing or have their card information stolen.

⚠️ Warning Signs

Common Red Flags:

  • They try to make you panic so you act without thinking
  • They ask for card details, passwords, or security codes
  • They request gift cards, crypto, or hard-to-reverse payments
  • They send fake payments and demand money back
  • They discourage you from checking with your bank
  • Fake alerts trick you into responding quickly

How to Stay Safe

  • Check payment requests carefully
  • Never share card numbers or security codes through messages
  • Visit websites directly instead of clicking payment links
  • Use trusted payment methods
  • Contact companies using official phone numbers
